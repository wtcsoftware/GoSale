🎂 Baking Business
in a Box

Photo 1

Photo 2

Photo 3

A starter kit that teaches beginners how to turn baking into a small business — combining baking skills, business knowledge, branding, marketing, and real selling experience into one complete package.

1
🥐 Learn How to Bake a Sellable Product
Easy-to-follow recipes (cookies, cupcakes, small cakes) so beginners can gain baking confidence, practise following recipes, and produce a consistent, saleable product from day one.
2
💰 Understanding Costs & Pricing
Learn how much ingredients cost, how many products a recipe makes, how to set a selling price, and how to calculate profit — the foundations of any successful product business.
3
🎨 Creating a Brand Identity
Choose a business name, design a logo, select brand colours, and build a simple identity that makes your baking business stand out and feel truly yours.
4
📦 Packaging & Presentation
Discover how to package products neatly, label them professionally, and make baked goods look irresistible — because great presentation directly increases sales.
5
📱 Marketing Your Products
Find out where to sell (school, markets, church, neighbours, online), how to promote on Instagram, and how to take stunning product photos that attract customers.
6
🤝 Customer Experience
Learn how to communicate with customers, deliver a great product, and why reputation and repeat business matter — building trust that keeps customers coming back.
7
🚀 Real Entrepreneurial Experience
The most important concept: you actually bake a product, price it, brand it, and sell it. Real experience — not just theory — that prepares you for the real world of business.
